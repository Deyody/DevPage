Endothermic Pre-Workout Drink

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp. Ginger Powder
    Or Mashed Ginger Root, Pulp and Juice
  • 1 Tbsp. Cayenne
  • 1 Tbsp. Baking Soda, Leveled
  • 1 Tbsp. Honey
  • 1/2 Lemon, Juiced
  • 1/2 Tbsp. Turmeric
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Pinch of Pepper

Recipe

Mix all of these ingredients into about one water bottles worth of water and it will not taste good at all. Though it will raise your body temperature about another 20 degrees during workouts.
